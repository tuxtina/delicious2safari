Using delicious2safari

Basics

  1. Enter your del.icio.us login and password
  2. Adjust download settings (optional)
  3. Start the download

Important: Even though delicious2safari takes great care when updating your Safari bookmarks, there might be a situation where you would like to restore your old Safari bookmarks. delicious2safari always creates a backup of your Safari bookmarks in Library/Application Support/delicious2safari in your home directory.

Options

You can choose where to put your del.icio.us bookmarks in Safari's bookmark hierarchy:

  • Bookmarks (default): del.icio.us bookmarks will be placed in a folder named "del.icio.us" in the top-most level of your Safari bookmarks. To see or edit those bookmarks, choose "Show All Bookmarks" from Safari's bookmark menu.
  • Bookmarks Menu: del.icio.us bookmarks will be placed in a folder named "del.icio.us" in your Safari bookmarks menu.
  • Bookmarks Bar: del.icio.us bookmarks will be placed in a folder named "del.icio.us" in your Safari bookmarks bar.

If there are folders named "del.icio.us" in your chosen destination, you will be asked if you want to replace them (it is possible to have more than one folder with the same name in Safari). You can also set delicious2safari to always replace existing folders by checking Don't ask before replacing folders.

By default, your del.icio.us bookmarks will be downloaded as one flat list of bookmarks. If you want to have your del.icio.us bookmarks organized into subfolders according to their associated tags, check the Organize bookmarks according to tags option. This will slightly increase the time needed to process the downloaded bookmarks, depending on how many tags are associated with each bookmarks, and how many bookmarks you have.
